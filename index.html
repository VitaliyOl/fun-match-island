About the Game

Mergical invites you to a mystical island shrouded in mystery. Once a flourishing paradise, the island now lies under a dark spell. Using your merging skills and a touch of magic, revive the island and restore it to its former glory. Solve enchanting puzzles, awaken mythical creatures, and design a magical realm filled with beauty and wonder.
